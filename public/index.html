📡 How Live Streaming Works
1️⃣
Select your microphone from the dropdown. Click "Refresh Mics" if your device isn't listed. Grant browser permission when prompted.
2️⃣
Enter a title and speaker name. These appear on the listener player in real time.
3️⃣
Click GO LIVE. Your microphone audio streams instantly to all listeners at /live-stream — no external app needed.
4️⃣
Stop to return to scheduled playback. The listener player switches sources automatically.
💡 Tip: Use a headset or external microphone for best audio quality. The mic level meter shows you before going live.
